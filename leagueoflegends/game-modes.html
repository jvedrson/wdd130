League of Legends contains a variety of game modes that can offer you different gaming experiences. These are some of the most popular game modes at the moment:

Classic Mode

Classic Mode is the main mode of League of Legends. Here, two teams of five players will enter the well-known Summoner's Rift, each team will compete against each other to destroy the opposing team's base. On the Rift map there are 3 lanes (Top, Mid and Bot) and a jungle in which you must destroy monsters to obtain gold, experience and buffs that will give you an advantage in the game. This mode requires teamwork, strategic thinking and individual skills. All this will lead you to victory or defeat.

ARAM (All Random All Mid)

In ARAM mode, two teams of five players each will compete in which they will fight and face each other in a single lane, Yes, just as you heard a single lane. There will be no options to retreat to the base to restore your champion's health nor will you be able to enter the jungle as it will not exist on this map. Champions are selected at random, so your level as a player will be tested, resulting in chaotic and unpredictable battles.

Teamfight Tactics (TFT)

Is a turn-based strategy game in the League of Legends universe. Here players form a team of champions and fight against other players in tactical battles. The goal is to be the last team standing.

Nexus Blitz

Nexus Blitz is a fast and chaotic game mode with various events and challenges. Two teams of five players each compete for victory as they advance across a condensed map.

News

League of legends is a game that is constantly being updated. Thus providing new expectations every year, so you can enjoy new environments both in the maps and in the game modes. Each update can bring with it the introduction of new champions, jungle monsters, store item updates, new skins for your champions, among others.

Yeah! You will enjoy new information in each update (patch) that will turn the game into total chaos as everyone competes and learns to maximize and power up their champions with the new available resources that the game provides.
