World Championship

The League of Legends World Championship is a professional tournament held annually in which the best teams from each region and league in the world compete. That's right, League of Legends is played worldwide by millions of players at a competitive level, in which the most outstanding players are hired by sports teams and companies that support electronic sports. Each team fights to participate in this championship, being there will give you the recognition and fame that you are looking for as a team and player, and winning this championship will not only give you that but the winner will receive a million-dollar reward in dollars his house.

History Worlds League of Legends

The event was first created in 2011 by the game's creators themselves, RIOT Games. The idea of the event was the same as today, bringing together the best players in the world to compete against each other, with a prize for the winning team of $50,000.

This first event was held at Dreamhack Summer 2011 in Jönköping, Sweden. The final of the first League of Legends World Cup pitted the two favorite sides: Europe and North America. Finally, the European team, Fnatic, managed to win the first League of Legends World Cup.

The second year is the turning point of the Worlds, where several of the most significant changes in its history occurred. The venue was in Los Angeles, United States, and more than five thousand people attended in person and a total of seven million viewers streamed. The prize for the winners of the second event was raised to one million dollars. For that event, what is known today as the 'Summoner's Cup' was created, which was commissioned by Thomas Lyte, a luxury goldsmith in charge of making high-profile sports trophies such as the Guinness Six Nations Rugby Cup or the Rugby Trophy the FA Cup of the Premier League.

One of the most important milestones in the history of the LoL Worlds occurred that year with the inclusion of Asian countries such as South Korea, China, and Taiwan. Since that year, no team outside of Asia has managed to win the rest of the LoL World Cups.
